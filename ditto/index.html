Double Ditto - Rules

  • Take turns being the Ditto Master by reading the card out loud to the group
  • Players then have 15 seconds to write down 2 answers that come to mind
  • Once time is up, take turns to read the answers out loud, Ditto Master goes last
  • Cross off each answer that matches Ditto Master's answer.
  • Score 1 point for 1 match (Shout Ditto)
  • Score 3 points for 2 matches (Shout Double Ditto)
